Get Meta! (Hire me.)

December 18, 2013 open-data

As you may have realized, I’ve recently been studying data about open data so that we can use data to drive our opening of data. Because data. Data, data, data!

I want to keep doing this research all day.

Things I can do for you

If you work for a data-related software company, I can prototype or implement new features in your product to help your users find value in open data. This could involve identification of out-of-date data and new ways of searching for data.

If you work for an organization that wants to release open data or share data internally, I can help you do that better. By collecting data about your data, I can figure out what’s working and what isn’t to help you decide what to do next. For example, I can determine what sorts of data you have, whether your datasets are licensed properly, and how people are using your data.

If you are giving grants in relation to open data, we should talk. I have a long list of things that I want to do.

Interested?
