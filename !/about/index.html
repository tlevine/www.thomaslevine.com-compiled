About Thomas Levine

Thomas Levine is a dada artist who whimsically moves now that his apartment fits in a small suitcase. He likes to sleep, and he’s usually curious about why people do things. He has recently been playing music, teaching data things, and studying how people share spreadsheets. His favorite color is pink.

History

Playing with computers since he was young, Thomas eventually developed back and wrist pain, and then he started studying ergonomics. His back and wrists now hurt less. At some point, people started calling him a data scientist.
