Peltor TacticalPro Electronic Hearing Protector - Collapsable Folding Headband MT15H7F-SV Product Info

The Peltor TacticalPro Hearing Protector MT15H7F-SV features a unique frequency response that amplifies signals and voices with near-perfect sound reproduction. The Tactical Pro Hearing Protector MT15H7FSV has an Active-Volume that provides maximum distortion-free amplification of low level sounds up to 18dB. The Tactical-Pro 's Omni-Directional Stereo Microphones provide 360° external ambient listening. These Peltor Hearing Protectors have a Noise Reduction Rating (NRR) of 26 decibels. In addition to the performance of the Peltor Tactical-Pro Hearing Protector MT15H7F SV is its comfort and design. Peltor TacticalPro Hearing Protectors have liquid/gel filled ear cushions and a folding stainless steel padded comfort band for extra comfort during long periods of use.

The Peltor TacticalPro Hearing Protector MT15H7F-SV is also available with a behind-the-head neckband design (model MT15H7B-SV) for cap and hat wearers.
