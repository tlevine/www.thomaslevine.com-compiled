Pro-Ears Pro 300 Shooting Hearing Protection Headsets Product Info

Pro-Ears Pro 300 Shooting Hearing Protection Headsets will increase enjoyment and improve performance for both the hunter and shooter. These Pro-Ears Hearing Protection Headsets offer industry leading compression technology, wind abatement programming and built-in quality, while the circuit board reduces high frequency filtering which may benefit those with high-frequency hearing loss. Pro Ears also offers leather ear cushions with visco-elastic foam ensures the highest level of sound attenuation and comfort available. An internal battery compartment eliminates unnecessary cuts or voids in the external cup, improving the noise attenuation properties of the cup. No tools are required to change the N size 1/2 volt battery.

Features of ProEars Pro 300 Hearing Protection Headsets:

  • Preset at 15 decibels of amplification for operation in a continuously noisy environment
  • No high frequency filtering to help those with high frequency hearing loss
  • Highly versatile cup with a multi-purpose design
  • Weight: 9.1 oz
  • Independently tested and certified
  • Most comprehensive warranty in the industry
