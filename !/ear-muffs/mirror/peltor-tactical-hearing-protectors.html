3M Peltor 6S Tactical Hearing Protection Ear Muffs Product Info

Peltor Tactical 6-S SoundTrap Hearing Protection is ideal for applications where a slim line cup is required. The electronic vent on these Peltor Hearing Protectors reduce hazardous impulse noise from firearms to harmless levels to 82 dBA within five milliseconds. The separate microphone, receiver, amplifier and volume control in each cup gives the Tactical Sound Trap Hearing Protection from Peltor, true stereophonic reception leaving impulsive noise immediately attenuated. 3M Peltor Tactical 6S SoundTrap Earmuffs are the #1 selling tactical hearing protector, is best in class in comfort, performance and protection. The low profile earcups make it the choice of long gun shooters everywhere.

Features of 3M Peltor Tactical Sound Trap Hearing Protection:

  • NRR 19 dB
  • Headset for applications where a slim-line cup is required
  • Electronic limit amplified sounds to 82 dBA within 5 milliseconds
  • Active-volume provides distortion-free amplification of low level sounds up to 19 dBA
  • Stereo microphones provide enhanced sound detection
  • Independent volume controls on each cup
  • (4) AAA batteries provide 300+ hours of operation
  • Lightweight (8.8 oz.)
  • No audio accessory jack
