Scarsdale High School bus schedule weirdness

February 04, 2013

Scarsdale High School bus schedules are here. I want to make a map of them! And maybe do other things.

I manually transcribed the file because it’s a small messy scan. In my resulting spreadsheet, each row is a bus stop on a trip. (The trip from home to school and the trip from school to home are different trips.)
